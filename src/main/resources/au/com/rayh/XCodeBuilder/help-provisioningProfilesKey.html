When signing xcode9 Add the provisioningProfiles entry to the Plist file. To avoid the following error
Xcode9 sign error: exportArchive: "MyApp.app" requires a provisioning profile.Error Domain=IDEProvisioningErrorDomain Code=9 .

Additional plist
<key>provisioningProfiles</key>
<dict>
<key>${PROVISIONINGPROFILES_KEY</key>
<string>${PROVISIONINGPROFILES_VALUE}</string>
</dict>
